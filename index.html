Why Choose TathyaUniverse?

Real-time Updates

Our platform provides the latest IPO details including company names, issue dates, closing dates, price bands, and even company logos — updated instantly from the backend.

Indian Market Focus

TathyaUniverse is built specifically for the Indian IPO market. All features are designed around Indian regulations, investor needs, and stock market trends.

Secure Applications

Powered by Django and PostgreSQL, the system ensures strong security with CSRF protection, input validation, and restricted file uploads — giving users trustworthy IPO data.

Seamless User Experience

Investors can access IPO information directly from the homepage without needing to register or log in. Data is displayed in a clean and easy-to-read format for quick decision-making.

Verified & Managed Data

IPO details are added and updated only through the secure Django admin panel. This ensures accuracy, authenticity, and up-to-date records for all IPOs listed.

API Support for Developers

Through our built-in REST API, external applications and developers can directly fetch IPO data. This makes TathyaUniverse not just a platform for investors, but also a valuable data source for fintech apps.

Indian IPO Market Insights

Record Year for IPOs

2024 has seen unprecedented IPO activity with over 50 companies raising ₹75,000+ crores, marking the highest fundraising year in Indian capital markets history.

Retail Participation Growth

Retail investor participation has grown by 180% compared to 2023, with average application values increasing significantly across all IPO categories.

Tech Dominance

Technology and fintech companies continue to dominate the IPO landscape, accounting for 35% of total issue size and commanding premium valuations.

Average Listing Gains

IPOs in 2024 have delivered an average listing gain of 22%, with mainboard issues outperforming SME segment consistently.

Top Performers

Leading performers include renewable energy, pharmaceutical, and technology companies, with some delivering over 90% listing gains.

Long-term Returns

12-month post-listing analysis shows 68% of 2023 IPOs trading above their issue price, indicating strong fundamental performance.

Technology Sector

IT services and software companies continue to attract premium valuations, with average P/E ratios of 28-35x reflecting strong growth expectations.

Financial Services

NBFC and fintech IPOs have shown consistent performance with stable valuations and strong institutional interest from both domestic and foreign investors.

Renewable Energy

Clean energy companies are commanding significant investor interest, supported by government policy initiatives and ESG investment trends.
